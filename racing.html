Fast & Furious Showdown UI Redesigning

About Project

The process of creating a user interface design on Playstation is typically centered on addressing an issue or assisting people in meeting their requirements. I decided to take the old version of the Fast & Furious Showdown racing game to redesign the entire UI.

Fast & Furious: Showdown is a cooperative racing game, based on the Fast & Furious franchise. Developed by Firebrand Games and published by Activision for Microsoft Windows, Playstation 3, Xbox 360, the game’s story ties into the events in Fast & Furious 6, including bridging the events between the story of the film. The game was released on May 21, 2013.


The game is listed in the top 10 worst racing games of the decades. (According to the Metacritic and customer review). Fast & Furious: Showdown was a game that both critics and gamers seemed to agree was a horrible game. The graphics were terrible for a Playstation 3 title about cars, the controls were chunky, the missions were bland and basic, and there was little to do in this game. Very few players felt it was worth wasting time on and lamented that the fun franchise had fallen to such a low point.
