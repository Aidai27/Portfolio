Problem Statement

Project Objective

When visiting most of the sites dedicated to air tickets, the user faces some problems.


For example:

  • Usability concerns with UI controls such as location picker, date picker, color contrast, accessibility, and so on.
  • There is an excessive amount of information being requested that is unrelated to business.
  • The design makes you feel overwhelmed.
  • Too many unnecessary advertisements.
  • The search results are quite muddled, particularly for return flights and multi-destination possibilities.
  • The website navigation is really inefficient, and content is organized primarily on business logic rather than the user.
  • Lengthy processing steps that need to be performed.

Research

My Role

I started by looking at the websites of other similar airlines and considering the advantages and disadvantages. After that, I conducted interviews with potential consumers to find out about their wants and needs.

I was in charge of Research, Sketch Ideation, UI Design, Persona interviews, Creating Customer Journey Maps, Low/High fidelity Wireframes, and Flow Charts.

I lead the Visual Interface Design, Interactivity, and UI Prototyping.

Duration

Despite the project’s difficulty, the site was created, tested, and launched in less than 10 weeks.
